Objects: 

Access: O(1)
Search: O(N)
Insert: O(1)
Unordered

Arrays:

Access: O(1)
Search: O(N)
Insert: Dependends on position of insertion. Insertion at the end - O(1). Insertion at the beginning - O(N).
Ordered
Apart from insert, push and pop almost all the javascript functions on arrays fall under O(N).
